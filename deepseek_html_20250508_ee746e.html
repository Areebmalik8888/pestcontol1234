About Beauty Kind Foundation

Learn about our mission and values

Our Mission

Founded in 2015, the Beauty Kind Foundation (EIN: 47-1864930) is committed to eliminating health hazards caused by pests in underserved communities. We believe everyone deserves to live in a safe, pest-free environment regardless of their financial situation.

What We Do

We provide professional-grade pest control services at no cost to qualifying households. Our licensed technicians use environmentally-friendly methods to address infestations while educating communities on prevention techniques.
